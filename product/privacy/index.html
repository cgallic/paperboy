Privacy notice

Your feeds and focus stay yours.

Effective July 16, 2026

What Paperboy collects

Paperboy stores the email address, public feed URLs, relevance instructions, ignore terms, time zone, delivery status, and campaign attribution you submit. It also keeps the minimum operational records needed to send editions, honor unsubscribe requests, prevent abuse, and diagnose failures.

How it is used

The data is used to validate public feeds, rank feed items, send the brief and service messages, manage the subscription, process payment through hosted checkout, and understand product performance when optional analytics consent is enabled. If you opt in, those analytics can include anonymous website lifecycle events and edition opens or source-link clicks. Without that opt-in, editions contain direct source links and no open pixel.

What Paperboy does not access

Paperboy does not connect to Gmail or read your inbox. It reads only the public RSS and Atom URLs you provide. The website does not load advertising pixels or third-party analytics scripts.

Service providers and payment

Email, hosting, and payment providers process only the information needed to provide their service. Card details are entered on Stripe-hosted checkout and are not stored by Paperboy.

Control and deletion

Every edition includes a way to unsubscribe. Use the private management link to stop delivery. To request access, correction, or deletion, reply to a Paperboy service email. Operational and legal records may be retained where required.

Security

Confirmation and management links are private credentials. Do not share them. Paperboy uses reasonable technical safeguards, but no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Operator and contact

Paperboy is operated by Connor Gallic. Privacy and account requests can be sent to paperboy@kaibuilds.com.
